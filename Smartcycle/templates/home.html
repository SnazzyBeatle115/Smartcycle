Recycling Case Studies

Upcycled Planters:

Transform old containers, tin cans, or plastic bottles into unique planters for your indoor or outdoor garden. Paint and decorate them for a personalized touch.

Repurposed Magazine Art:

Create colorful and abstract art by cutting and weaving strips of old magazines or newspapers. Frame the finished pieces to add a burst of creativity to your walls.

Tire Swing:

Turn old tires into a classic backyard swing for kids. Paint them in vibrant colors or leave them natural for a rustic look. Hang the tire swing from a sturdy tree branch using strong rope.

Bottle Cap Mosaic:

Collect and sort bottle caps by color to create mosaic designs. Glue them onto a wooden board or any suitable surface to make unique and eye-catching wall art.

Plastic Bag Crochet:

Repurpose plastic bags into yarn by cutting them into strips. Use the "plarn" to crochet reusable tote bags, doormats, or even hats.

Wine Cork Bulletin Board:

Save wine corks and glue them onto a board to create a stylish and functional bulletin board. It's a great way to display notes, photos, and reminders while adding a touch of character to your space.
